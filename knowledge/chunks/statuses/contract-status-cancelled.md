**Status:** CANCELLED (Cancelled)

**Business Meaning:** The contract was cancelled before or during activation. May result from manual cancellation, D-Country rejection, or auto-cancellation rules.

**When This Status Occurs:** Status is set when the contract or amendment reaches this stage in the order lifecycle. Most transitions after submission are driven by Salesforce/Middleware events, not direct dealer action.

**Allowed Actions:** View cancellation reason, Create new contract if needed

**Restricted Actions:** Reactivate

**Possible Next Statuses:** Terminal or workflow-dependent

**Typical User Questions:** What does Cancelled mean? Why is my contract in Cancelled status? How long will it stay in Cancelled?

**Example Support Response:** Your contract is currently in Cancelled status. The contract was cancelled before or during activation. May result from manual cancellation, D-Country rejection, or auto-cancellation rules. If you need to take action, you can: View cancellation reason, Create new contract if needed.
