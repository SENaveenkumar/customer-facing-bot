**Status:** AMENDMENT_ON_HOLD (Amendment On Hold)

**Business Meaning:** The amendment is blocked by order management, often for price change approval.

**When This Status Occurs:** Status is set when the contract or amendment reaches this stage in the order lifecycle. Most transitions after submission are driven by Salesforce/Middleware events, not direct dealer action.

**Allowed Actions:** Approve and resubmit pricing, View hold reason

**Restricted Actions:** None specific to this status

**Possible Next Statuses:** AMENDMENT_SUBMITTED, AMENDMENT_ACTIVATION_IN_PROGRESS, AMENDMENT_CANCELLED

**Typical User Questions:** What does Amendment On Hold mean? Why is my contract in Amendment On Hold status? How long will it stay in Amendment On Hold?

**Example Support Response:** Your contract is currently in Amendment On Hold status. The amendment is blocked by order management, often for price change approval. If you need to take action, you can: Approve and resubmit pricing, View hold reason.
