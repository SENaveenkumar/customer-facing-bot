**Status:** AMENDMENT_SUBMITTED (Amendment Submitted)

**Business Meaning:** The dealer submitted an amendment order for processing.

**When This Status Occurs:** Status is set when the contract or amendment reaches this stage in the order lifecycle. Most transitions after submission are driven by Salesforce/Middleware events, not direct dealer action.

**Allowed Actions:** Monitor status, Cancel amendment if needed

**Restricted Actions:** None specific to this status

**Possible Next Statuses:** AMENDMENT_ON_HOLD, AMENDMENT_ACTIVATION_IN_PROGRESS, AMENDMENT_ACTIVATED

**Typical User Questions:** What does Amendment Submitted mean? Why is my contract in Amendment Submitted status? How long will it stay in Amendment Submitted?

**Example Support Response:** Your contract is currently in Amendment Submitted status. The dealer submitted an amendment order for processing. If you need to take action, you can: Monitor status, Cancel amendment if needed.
