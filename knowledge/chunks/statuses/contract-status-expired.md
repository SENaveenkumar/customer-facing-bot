**Status:** EXPIRED (Expired)

**Business Meaning:** The contract draft expired before submission. Draft quotes are valid for 30 days.

**When This Status Occurs:** Status is set when the contract or amendment reaches this stage in the order lifecycle. Most transitions after submission are driven by Salesforce/Middleware events, not direct dealer action.

**Allowed Actions:** Create new contract, Start renewal if previously activated contract expired

**Restricted Actions:** Submit expired draft

**Possible Next Statuses:** DRAFT recreation

**Typical User Questions:** What does Expired mean? Why is my contract in Expired status? How long will it stay in Expired?

**Example Support Response:** Your contract is currently in Expired status. The contract draft expired before submission. Draft quotes are valid for 30 days. If you need to take action, you can: Create new contract, Start renewal if previously activated contract expired.
